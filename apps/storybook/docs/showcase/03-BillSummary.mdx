# Bill Summary

The coupon-aware total-payable panel on the booking-confirmation and
completed-record screens. Line items on top, coupon badge in the middle,
total anchored at the bottom in the brand green so the payable number is
what the eye lands on.

**Built from:** `Card` · `Card.Divider` · `Text` · tokens (`greenPale`,
`greenDark`)
