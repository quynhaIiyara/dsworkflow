# Request Card

The pending-request row for the accept flow. Farmer, service, location, and
requested date, with a slot-availability banner derived from the date. The
whole card taps through to the request detail; the **Select new date** button
appears only when the day is busy or unavailable.

Rendered to match the source in `design/design-system/docs-site/shell.html`
(`.rqc`) pixel-for-pixel.

**Built from:** icons (`LeafIcon`, `MapPinIcon`, `CalendarIcon`,
`CheckCircleIcon`, `AlertIcon`, `ChevronRight`) + local `StatusChip`,
`MetaRow`, `SlotBanner`, and `SelectNewDateButton` compositions (inline).

## Preview

## States

The slot-availability banner is derived from the requested date. A busy or
unavailable day also surfaces the **Select new date** action. An overdue
request paints the date row red and swaps the chip to the overdue tone.
